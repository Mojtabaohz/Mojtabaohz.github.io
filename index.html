Magrid

2021 - 2022

Magrid is an educational mobile app that empowers young learners with its innovative approach to visual learning. I spearheaded the development and optimization of the app on Unity, ensuring its availability across multiple platforms including iOS and Android. Northern Farm

2017-2019

Northern Farm was a unique farming simulation game with a distinctive Persian theme, featuring localized items, ingredients, and engaging mini-games. The project included the development of social features, such as clans and item trading, as well as special themed live events to enhance player engagement and retention. As part of the design and development team, I focused on improving key performance indicators (KPIs) and revenue through data-driven strategies. These included implementing A/B tests, analyzing player behavior, and introducing features to optimize the game’s lifecycle and maximize player satisfaction. Though no longer available, Northern Farm remains a highlight of my career as it combined cultural elements with innovative gameplay, leaving a lasting impression on its audience.

Tactics

2017 - 2019

Tactics was an innovative freemium mobile strategy game that blended elements of collectible card games and base defense strategy. Players could strategically build and customize their defenses while assembling decks of troops and buff cards to launch attacks on other players or progress through challenging campaign levels. As part of the development process, I contributed to crafting a gameplay experience that balanced strategic depth with accessibility, ensuring player engagement through a combination of tactical decision-making and progression mechanics. Although the project was ultimately unreleased, it demonstrated a novel approach to combining two popular genres and remains a valuable learning experience in designing unique and engaging player experiences.

Glass Garden

2017

Glass Garden wins third position during the SABA event in Iran. Glass Garden was a farming management game in which you plant and maintain different kinds of flowers. Using the application you can virtually grow different plants and learn how to satisfy the different needs of each plant. Controlling different attributes like the amount of sunlight or water and airflow requirements for each plant. The goal of the application was to help people to keep their plant needs on watch and maintain their plant as best using the application guidance.

About Me

I am a passionate game designer and Unity developer with over seven years of experience in the gaming and technology industry. My journey has been defined by a blend of technical expertise, creativity, and a relentless curiosity for understanding user behavior. From leading the design of engaging game economies to crafting immersive augmented reality applications, I thrive at the intersection of innovation and player-centric design. I am deeply committed to mentoring and community building, demonstrated through my active role as a board member for the Groningen Global Game Jam and years of mentoring aspiring developers. My collaborative spirit and dedication to data-driven solutions have allowed me to enhance user experiences, optimize KPIs, and drive the success of numerous projects. Whether it’s developing AR tours, improving live game services, or exploring innovative gameplay mechanics, I approach every challenge with enthusiasm and a focus on delivering meaningful experiences. Outside of work, I enjoy analyzing complex game systems, hosting local game nights, and immersing myself in strategic and simulation games that fuel my creativity. I am always eager to connect, learn, and contribute to projects that push the boundaries of interactive technology.
